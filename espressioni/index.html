Espressioni Quotidiane — Everyday Expressions
Essential phrases for daily life in Italy — greetings, polite expressions, reactions, and conversational fillers that make you sound natural.
Greetings & Goodbyes
Italian greetings change based on formality and time of day. Use Lei (formal "you") with strangers, elders, and in professional settings.

Al Ristorante — Food & Dining Expressions
Italy revolves around food. These expressions will help you navigate restaurants, bars, markets, and kitchen conversations like a local.
Ordering & Dining
At a restaurant, the waiter may say "Prego?" (meaning "What would you like?"). You can start with "Vorrei..." (I would like) or "Prendo..." (I'll have).
ItalianoEnglishWhen

Prendo...I'll have...Deciding
Per me...For me...In a group
Il conto, per favoreThe check, pleaseReady to pay
Posso avere...?Can I have...?Polite request
Che cosa ci consiglia?What do you recommend?Asking the waiter
Buon appetito!Enjoy your meal!Before eating
Cin cin! / Salute!Cheers!Toasting
Basta così, grazieThat's enough, thanksDeclining more food
È squisito!It's delicious!Complimenting food
